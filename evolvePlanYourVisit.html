Evolve Kids

to Sunday Too

Evolve Kids is a world-class children's ministry built on the belief that kids can have a genuine encounter with God — in their own language.

Secure check-in system — every child, every service

Age-specific curriculum — not just babysitting

Pickup tag system — parents get a tag at check-in

Pager alert — we'll reach you instantly if needed

Ages Birth – 2
Nursery
A calm, safe space for your littlest ones with trained care workers.
Ages 3 – 5
Preschool
Play, stories, and big truths in a small-world format they understand.
K – 2nd Grade
Lower Elementary
High-energy worship, games, and lessons that spark faith.
3rd – 5th Grade
Upper Elementary
Real conversations, real faith, designed for kids who are asking real questions.

Never. We know walking into a new church can feel vulnerable. You will not be singled out, asked to raise your hand, or put on the spot in any way.

You're exactly who we're thinking about when we prepare each week. No prior knowledge required. Pastor Ken teaches in a way that meets you right where you are.

We have reserved "First Time Guest" parking spots close to the entrance. Look for the signage and our parking team will point you right to them.

Yes! We stream live at evolvenation.org every Sunday at 9 AM, 10:30 AM, and 12 PM. It's a great way to check us out before you visit in person — no signup needed.

Free coffee, always. It's one of the first things people notice and one of the most important things we do. Come early and grab a cup before you find your seat.

Head to the Connect Table after service — our team will walk you through small groups, volunteer opportunities, and next steps. Or visit evolvenation.org to explore on your own time.

A church for Milwaukee and beyond. Real people, real faith, real community — every Sunday.
