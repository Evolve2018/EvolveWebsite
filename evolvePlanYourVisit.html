First Time?

What to Expect

We want your first experience at Evolve to be one you'll remember — in the best way possible.

Worship

Expect live, energetic worship music that creates space to connect with God. Our worship team leads with passion and authenticity every week.

Message

Relevant, scripture-based messages from Pastor Ken designed to meet you right where you are — honest, hopeful, and practical for everyday life.

Community

From the parking lot to the lobby, you'll be welcomed by real people who are genuinely glad you showed up. We're a church family — come as you are.

About 75 Minutes

Our services run about 75 minutes. We start on time and we respect yours — but we hope you'll want to stay and connect afterward.
