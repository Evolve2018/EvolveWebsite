Who Is Jesus?

More Than a Good Teacher

Jesus of Nazareth lived over 2,000 years ago, yet His life divided history and His name is known in every corner of the world. He claimed to be not just a prophet or teacher — but the Son of God, the Savior of humanity.

"I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me." — John 14:6

Unlike any other religious figure, Jesus backed His claims with miracles, an empty tomb, and the transformed lives of billions across every generation. Knowing Him changes everything.

What the Bible Says

Three Truths
That Change Everything

01
We All Fall Short

Every person has sinned — fallen short of God's perfect standard. This sin creates a separation between us and God that we cannot bridge on our own.

Romans 3:23 · Romans 6:23
02
God Came for Us

God loved us too much to leave us separated. He sent His Son Jesus, who lived a perfect life and died on the cross to pay the penalty for our sin.

John 3:16 · Romans 5:8
03
New Life Is Available

Jesus rose from the dead, defeating sin and death. Anyone who places their faith in Him receives forgiveness, new life, and the promise of eternity with God.

Romans 10:9–10 · Ephesians 2:8–9
How to Respond

Beginning a Relationship
with Jesus

Becoming a Christian isn't about being good enough or having it all together. It's about responding to what Jesus has already done. Here's what that looks like:

1
Acknowledge

Honestly recognize that you have sinned and that your sin has separated you from God. This isn't about shame — it's about seeing the truth clearly so healing can begin.

2
Believe

Put your trust in Jesus Christ — that He is the Son of God, that He died for your sins, and that God raised Him from the dead on the third day. This is the foundation of the Christian faith.

3
Confess & Commit

Confess Jesus as Lord of your life — not just Savior. Invite Him into your heart and make the decision to follow Him. This is a relationship, not just a religion, and it begins with a simple, sincere prayer.

A Simple Prayer

Ready to Begin?

If you've never given your life to Jesus — or if you want to recommit — you can pray something like this from your heart right now. God hears every sincere prayer.

Lord Jesus, I know I am a sinner. I believe You died for my sins and rose again from the dead. Right now I turn from my sins and open the door of my heart and life. I receive You as my personal Lord and Savior. Thank You for saving me.

Words matter far less than sincerity. Pray in your own words.
